Dice Rollers

Basic Dice Roller

Roll basic polyhedral dice including d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20, and d100. Choose the number of dice and type, perfect for tabletop RPGs and board games.

Custom Dice Roller

Roll custom dice with arbitrary values. Enter any number of dice and any number of sides using text input. Supports both numeric input (e.g., 20) and dice notation (e.g., d20). Defaults to 3d6.

Fate Dice Roller

Roll Fate/Fudge dice (4dF). Each die shows plus (+), minus (-), or blank, with results ranging from -4 to +4. Designed for Fate Core and Fate Accelerated RPG systems.

About

This is a collection of online dice rollers for tabletop gaming. Each roller is designed for specific gaming systems and keeps a history of your rolls during your session.

More dice rollers will be added in the future to support additional gaming systems and mechanics.
